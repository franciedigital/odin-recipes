Golden Butter Rice.

Description

Indulge in the rich flavors of this delectable Golden Butter Rice recipe. Each grain of rice is infused with a luxurious blend of butter,aromatic spices, and fragrant herbs. 
   The result is a dish that's both comforting and irresistibly flavorful. Whether served as a side dish or enjoyed on its own, this Golden Butter Rice is sure to delight your taste buds and become a new favorite in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • long-grain white rice
  • unsalted butter
  • ground tumeric
  • ground cumin
  • ground coriander
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • chicken or vegetable broth
  • salt, to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • chopped fresh parsley or cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Steps

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to prepare Golden Butter Rice:

  1. Rinse the rice:place the rice in a fine-mesh sieve and rinse it under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps to remove excess starch and prevents the rice from becoming sticky.
  2. Melt the butter:In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat.
  3. Toast the spices:Once the butter is melted, add the ground tumeric, ground cumin, ground coriander, garlic powder, and onion powder to the saucepan.Toast the spices in the butter for about 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until fragrant. This helps release their flavors and aromas.
  4. Add the Rice: Add the rinsed rice to the saucepan with the toasted spices and butter. Stir the rice to coat it evenly with the butter and spices.
  5. Add the Broth:Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the saucepan with the rice. Stir to combine.
  6. Season:Season the rice with salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste.
  7. Bring to a Boil:Increase te heat to high and bring the mixture to a boil.
  8. Simmer:Once the mixture is boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a tight-lifting lid.Aloow the rice to simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  9. Fluff the Rice:Once the rice is cooked, remove the saucepan from the heat and let it sit, cover for about 5 minutes. Then, use a fork to fluff the rice to seperates the grains
  10. Serve:Transfer the Golden Butter Rice to a serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh parsely or cilantro, if desired. Serve hot as a delicious side dish or enjoy on its own.
